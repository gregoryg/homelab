* Food
** Mountainous Noodles - Peanutty Noodles
   + 1 lb. thin fresh noodles
   + cooking oil
   + 4 tbs. sesame oil
   + 1/4 cup crunchy peanut butter
   + 1 clove garlic, mashed
   + 1/4 cup light soy sauce
   + 2 tbs. Tabasco or pepper sauce
   + 1/2 cup malt or white vinegar
   + 1 tbs. Worcestershire sauce

   Cook noodles in plenty of boiling water, adding 1 tbs cooking oil for each 2 quarts of
   water. When noodles are done drain, rinse with tap water and drain well.

   Heat 2 tbs of the sesame oil and mix into drained noodles.  Chill at least 1 hour.

   Blend remaining sesame oil and peanut butter into a paste.  Add garlic, soy sauce,
   Tabasco, vinegar and Worcestershire sauce.

   Stir until well blended.

   Serve sauce in separate bowl

** Margarita El Presidente style
   + 2 oz blanco tequila
   + .5 oz Orange Curacao or other orange liqueur
   + 1 oz lime juice
   + .5 oz Chambord (OR LESS)
** Grapefruit Margarita
   + 2 ounces freshly-squeezed grapefruit juice
   + 1 1/2 ounces tequila, silver or reposado
   + 3/4 ounce Cointreau (or any orange liqueur)
   + 3/4 ounce freshly-squeezed lime juice
   + agave nectar for sweetening, to taste
   + ice
   + optional: lime wedge and salt for rimming the glass

** Cynar Sour
   + 2 ounces of Cynar
   + 1/4 ounce of Luxardo Maraschino liqueur
   + 1 ounce of freshly squeezed lemon juice
   + 1/4 ounce of agave nectar
   + 1 egg white

     Shake with ice for 1 minute+

     Strain into a second shaker without ice, shake for 30 seconds+
